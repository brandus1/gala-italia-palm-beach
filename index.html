The Event

An Exclusive Evening Celebrating Italian Excellence

For the first time, Gala Italia crosses the Atlantic to bring the finest of Italian culture, art, and lifestyle to one of America's most prestigious destinations. Organized by All About Italy — the international magazine promoting Italian excellence since 1997 — this landmark evening takes place on the eve of the Palm Beach International Boat Show.


An evening where art meets music, where design meets gastronomy, and where the spirit of Made in Italy comes alive under the roof of one of Florida's most celebrated cultural institutions.
