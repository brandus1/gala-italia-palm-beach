The Event

An Evening of Art, Culture, and Italian Lifestyle

Since 1997, All About Italy has served as an ambassador of Italian excellence worldwide, celebrating the authenticity of Made in Italy through prestigious events and international publications.


The Gala Italia offers a full-immersion experience that blends the elegance of Italian tradition with the contemporary energy of the industries that best represent Italy — hosted in a unique and prestigious setting: the Norton Museum of Art in Palm Beach, the night before the official opening of the Palm Beach International Boat Show.

Music & Heritage

Fondazione Luciano Pavarotti

A prestigious Italian music soirée by Fondazione Luciano Pavarotti — an exclusive and refined tribute to the timeless legacy of Maestro Luciano Pavarotti, featuring a curated selection of his most iconic and celebrated performances.


The evening also features a rare exhibition of the Maestro's personal treasures — including his iconic scarves, signature hats, and legendary stage costumes — alongside a strictly limited selection of autographed serigraphs, available for acquisition through a silent auction.

Culinary Excellence

The Refined Culinary Experience of BiCE Restaurant Group

A symbol of Italian excellence for over a century, the BiCE Restaurant Group represents the highest expression of refined Italian hospitality and gastronomy. Founded in Milan in 1926, the group celebrates its 100-year anniversary in 2026, honoring a legacy that began with the opening of the first BiCE Ristorante by Raffaele Ruggeri’s grandmother, a visionary whose passion laid the foundation for an enduring culinary institution.


Rooted in tradition, the BiCE Restaurant Group has become synonymous with timeless elegance, artisanal craftsmanship, and authentic Italian cuisine — values that continue to define its presence on the global culinary stage.


For Gala Italia, Salute Ristorante and BiCE Ristorante will present an evening of elevated gastronomic excellence in Palm Beach. The menu will be a bespoke celebration of Italian cuisine — deeply rooted in heritage, artfully refined, and crafted with uncompromising attention to quality and authenticity.

The Venue

Norton Museum of Art

Palm Beach was chosen for its unparalleled profile: it stands among the most exclusive and affluent communities in the United States. Renowned for its private clubs, charity galas, and prestigious auctions, Palm Beach thrives during its vibrant winter social season, attracting the international elite.


This privileged setting provides the perfect stage on the eve of the Palm Beach International Boat Show, one of America’s premier luxury yachting events. Gala Italia extends a warm welcome to the Italian yachting brands that will officially open the show on March 25th — celebrating the excellence of Made in Italy on the international stage.

The Organizer

All About Italy

Since 1997, All About Italy has been promoting Italian excellence worldwide, sharing authentic content through a strong editorial presence in the USA and Germany. The international platform highlights Italy’s excellence in culture, industry, and innovation.


All About Italy has grown into a multichannel network that combines high-quality content with strategic distribution across the key sectors of Made in Italy — lifestyle, food & wine, fashion, tourism, design, automotive, yachting, and culture.


With an experiential and relationship-driven editorial approach, All About Italy creates synergy between brands, institutions, artists, and entrepreneurs, serving as a cultural and commercial bridge between Italy, the United States, and Germany.
